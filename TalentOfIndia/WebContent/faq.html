Faq's

WHO CAN APPLY FOR AUDITIONS?

Participants from North India side only can apply for this audition. The registration process is online.

HOW TO APPLY ONLINE?

You need to register yourself online with complete information as mentioned in the form. Click on the Registration Button and fill all the details and upload your valid id (Adhaar Card, Voter Id) along with your valid signatures and pay amount online.

AUDITION CITIES?

We have shortlisted 10 cities all over the Punjab to select best talents. Selected cities are Patiala, Amritsar, Kapurthala, Firozpur, Ludhiana, Nangal, Chandigarh, Pathankot, Sangrur, Bathinda.

AUDITION PROCESS?

First audition will be conducted at your cities. After selection of 20 candidates from each city and they will invite to Ludhiana for Mega event.

WHAT IS THE VENUE OF AUDITIONS?

Venue details will be shared 1 month before with selected participants only via Call | SMS | E-Mail | Whatsapp etc.

WHAT IS THE LAST DATE FOR REGISTRATION?

The online registration will be closed before 2 days of Venue announcement. Offline Registration will not allowed. For Venue or any other update will be shared on TALENT OF INDIA Facebook & Website. Stay tuned with TALENT OF INDIA.

WHICH TV CHANNEL THE SHOW WILL BE TELECAST?

This is India's Finest T.V show so it will be telecast on our Media Parter Fastway& on our Youtube channel only and we will promote our contestants through Social Media Activities on Facebook, Twitter, Instagram, Google + etc.

WHY REGISTRATION FEE?

To avoid unwanted crowd at time of auditions.

WHO ARE THE JUDGES?

The judges are not popular known celebrities but we have music experts for the fair judgment. We believe to invest our funds to promoting our contestants rather than hiring expensive celebrity judges.

WHAT IS THE QUALIFICATION / ELIGIBILITY REQUIRED FOR THE PARTICIPATION?

There is no qualification or special training needed.

WHAT IS THE AGE LIMIT FOR THE PARTICIPATION?

To participate in TALENT OF INDIA your age must be 15-40 years.

WHAT ARE THE PRIZES FOR THE WINNERS?

First Prize T-series (Track Record With Video) 1
Second Prize Other Track Only Audio 1
Third Prize Cash Prize* *

Audience will select 3 stars by voting and first winner will get chance to record music video produced byT-SERISE. Music Videos will be recorded by professional cinematographers and music directors. Second runner up will get a chance to record one audio track with well reputed music company and the third runner up will get Cash amount of Rs - *. Contestants (Videos Bites) will be directly upload to on our official pages. Best performer will be known everywhere and talented Contestants will get their name fame amongst the unknown people as well.

WHAT AFTER COMPETITION?

Music Video's will be recorded by the professional cinematographers and it will be uploaded to our YouTube channel and after that we will promote it Online Through Social Media Activities to get Likes and Views to the Videos and the Winners will be known everywhere, and winners will get their name fame amongst the unknown people as well.

WHAT IS THE COMPETITION STORY AND SELECTION CRITERIA?

There will be 3 rounds in the whole competition to find out the deserving participants.

1st Round: Vocal / Voice Testing.

2nd Round: With Music Track (Rhythm Testing)

Grand Finale: Live Performance (Music Videos Shoot)

WHAT TYPE OF SONGS / GENRE IS REQUIRED IN THE COMPETITION?

You can sing any kind of Bollywood / Classical / Western / Punjabi / Hindi songs of your choice.

WHAT IS THE REGISTRATION FEE FOR AUDITIONS?

Rs. 1000/- only. (Non-Refundable)
